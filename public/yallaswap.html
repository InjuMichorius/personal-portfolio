Brandstov is a company where I have had the pleasure of experimenting web development with college friends. It arose from a desire to remove digital ambiguities and the will to create beautiful, user-friendly solutions.

Frontend Design Strategy

From 2022 to 2023 I worked with Joppe Pelgrim and David van Rumpt on various clients on behalf of Brandstov. During this period I invested a lot of time in researching and practicing web development, because I was responsible for the technical side of Branstov.

In a team of three, each with our own strengths (Storytelling, Design and Development), we built Brandstov and our future vision together. We looked for a challenge and pushed the boundaries of the web.

Once Joppe had his solution designed by David, I started working on it technically. I translated the design to web and took responsive web design, CMS usage and animations into account.

At brandstov I learned to work with Wordpress, Webflow, Umbraco and Squarespace and increased my knowledge of HTML, CSS and Javascript. Below are some designs made by David:

After we built a website, it was time to see how we could use our skills to help people increase their digital presence. Through an acquaintance of David we ended up at Handoff, a company owned by Dennis Petri that has a lot of experience with freelance work.

Working under the name Handoff for a while allowed us to have an impact on real customers, without much risk and with room to experiment. Below you will find a number of designs, sketches and concepts that we have provided for Handoff.

Although everything shown here is rough and has not yet reached its full potential, it gave us a clear idea of where our passions lay. Building cool things and giving new experiences.

All these designs are made in vanilla HTML, CSS Javascript, some using API's and libraries
